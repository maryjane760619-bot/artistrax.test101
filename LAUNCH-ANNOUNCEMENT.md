## Press Release (For Media Outlets)

### Headline
**Siesta Records Launches artistrax: The Fair-Pay Music Platform Where Artists Keep 95% and Fans Own + Stream Forever**

### Subheadline
New music platform reimagines ownership with "Stream Your Purchases" feature, PWA app, and 2% cashback rewards for fans

### Date
[LAUNCH DATE] — Encinitas, CA

---

### Body

**Encinitas, CA** — Today, Siesta Records announces the launch of **artistrax**, a new music distribution platform built to fix streaming's broken economics. Artists keep 95% of revenue (vs Spotify's 0.3% and Beatport's 70%), and fans can own music forever while also streaming it unlimited times.

"The streaming model is broken," said **Bert Porcayo, CEO of Siesta Records and founder of artistrax**. "Artists make pennies. Fans don't own anything. Labels take massive cuts. We built artistrax to change that. Fair pay for artists. True ownership for fans. Simple."

**artistrax: Where an artist can be an artist**

#### **Key Features**

**For Artists:**
- **95% revenue share** — highest in the industry
- **30-day free trial** — then $20/mo or $96/year (save 60%)
- **Link-in-Bio included** — centralize presence across 13 platforms
- **Instant payouts** — no 30-60 day holds

**For Fans:**
- **Stream Your Purchases** — buy once, stream unlimited + download lossless
- **2% cashback rewards** — 10 points per dollar, 500 points = 1 free track
- **PWA app** — install in 10 seconds on iOS, Android, or desktop (no app store required)
- **Offline playback** — works without internet

**For Labels:**
- **90% revenue share** — better economics than any competitor
- **30-day free trial** — then $25/mo or $120/year (save 60%)
- **Batch upload** — manage entire catalog efficiently
- **Release management** — organize by release date, genre, catalog number
- **Artist collaboration** — tracks can belong to both label and artist

#### **Historic Catalog Launch**

To mark the launch, Siesta Records is releasing its **entire back catalog** — 60-70 releases spanning 15+ years — on artistrax. This is the first time these releases have been available digitally.

"Siesta Records has a deep history in electronic music," Porcayo said. "We've been sitting on this catalog for years, waiting for a platform that respects artists and music ownership. Now we built it ourselves."

The catalog includes releases from DJ Halo, [other artists], and pioneering electronic producers from the mid-2000s to today.

#### **Competitive Positioning**

artistrax enters a crowded market dominated by Beatport, Bandcamp, and streaming giants like Spotify and Apple Music. But it's the only platform that offers:
- Higher artist revenue share than any competitor (95% vs Beatport's 70%, Bandcamp's 82%)
- "Stream Your Purchases" model (own AND stream, no subscription)
- PWA app (install in seconds, no app store)
- Link-in-Bio feature (replace Linktree)
- Fan rewards (2% cashback)

"We're not trying to be Spotify," Porcayo said. "We're bringing back ownership. You buy music. You own it. You stream it forever. And artists actually get paid."

#### **Availability**

artistrax is live today at **[artistrax.com](https://artistrax.com)**.

Artists and labels can sign up for free and start uploading music immediately. Fans can browse, buy, and stream the Siesta Records catalog and music from independent artists worldwide.

The PWA app can be installed on any device in under 10 seconds (no app store required).

#### **About Siesta Records**

Siesta Records is an independent electronic music label based in Encinitas, California, founded by Bert Porcayo. For over 15 years, Siesta has released cutting-edge electronic music from artists worldwide, with a focus on house, techno, and progressive sounds.

#### **About artistrax**

artistrax is a fair-pay music distribution platform where artists keep 95% of revenue and fans own music forever while also streaming it unlimited times. Built by Siesta Records, artistrax combines the ownership model of Bandcamp with the convenience of streaming, powered by a PWA app and 2% cashback rewards.

**Where an artist can be an artist**

For more information, visit **[artistrax.com](https://artistrax.com)** or contact **support@artistrax.com**.
