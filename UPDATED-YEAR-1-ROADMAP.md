## Risk Mitigation

### Risk 1: Artists Don't Adopt Video
**Mitigation:**
- Make it super easy (one-click upload)
- Showcase success stories
- Offer launch incentive (first 50 videos featured)

### Risk 2: Bandwidth Costs Spike
**Mitigation:**
- Use Backblaze B2 (cheap: $0.01/GB)
- Monitor usage weekly
- Add usage caps if needed (1,000 views/month included)

### Risk 3: Video Quality Issues
**Mitigation:**
- Start with 1080p max (no 4K)
- Use proven tech (Mux for transcoding)
- Test extensively before launch

### Risk 4: Development Delays
**Mitigation:**
- Keep MVP simple (HTML5 video player)
- Phased rollout (free videos first, paid later)
- Buffer time built in (2 months for development)
