## Content Pillars (What to Post About)

### 1. **Platform Features** (Educational)
Teach people how artistrax works and why it's better.

Examples:
- "How Stream Your Purchases works (buy once, stream forever)"
- "Why 95% artist revenue share matters"
- "PWA vs Native App: Why PWA wins"
- "How to install artistrax on iOS in 10 seconds"
- "Link-in-Bio feature walkthrough"
- "2% cashback rewards explained"

### 2. **Artist Spotlights** (Community Building)
Feature artists who upload to artistrax.

Examples:
- "Meet [Artist Name]: [Genre] producer from [Location]"
- "New release: [Artist] drops [Track/EP] on artistrax"
- "Artist success story: [Artist] made $X in first month"
- "Behind the music: [Artist] talks about their latest release"
- Quote cards from artist interviews

### 3. **Siesta Records Throwbacks** (Nostalgia)
Leverage the historic catalog.

Examples:
- "#ThrowbackThursday: [Release] from [Year]"
- "This Week in Siesta History: [Release] turns [X] years old"
- "Deep cut: [Track] you might have missed"
- "The story behind [Release]"
- "Then and now: [Artist] then vs [Artist] now"

### 4. **Economics Education** (Value Proposition)
Explain why fair pay matters.

Examples:
- "How many Spotify streams = 1 artistrax sale?"
- "Where your $1.99 goes: Spotify vs Beatport vs artistrax"
- "The hidden cost of streaming"
- "Why ownership > renting music"
- "Artist revenue comparison chart"

### 5. **Fan Stories** (Social Proof)
Share how fans use artistrax.

Examples:
- "Fan of the week: [Username] has bought 50 tracks"
- "How [Fan] earned a free track with rewards points"
- "Playlist spotlight: [Fan's playlist]"
- Testimonials: "Why I switched from Spotify to artistrax"
- Screenshots of fan reviews/messages

### 6. **Platform Updates** (Transparency)
Share what you're building.

Examples:
- "Coming soon: [New feature]"
- "We shipped: [Feature] is now live"
- "Behind the scenes: Building [Feature]"
- "You asked, we listened: [Feature] based on feedback"
- Roadmap updates

### 7. **Music Industry Commentary** (Thought Leadership)
Position artistrax as a thought leader.

Examples:
- "Why the streaming model is broken"
- "The future of music ownership"
- "What artists really think about Spotify"
- "How PWAs will disrupt app stores"
- "The creator economy in music"

## Analytics to Track

### Key Metrics (Check Weekly)
- **Signups**: Artists, labels, fans
- **Uploads**: Tracks added
- **Purchases**: Total sales, GMV (Gross Merchandise Value)
- **Traffic**: Website visits, referral sources
- **Social**: Followers, engagement rate, reach
- **Ad performance**: CTR, CPC, conversions

### Success Indicators
- **Artist retention**: % of artists who upload >1 track
- **Fan retention**: % of fans who make >1 purchase
- **Referral rate**: % of organic signups (word of mouth)
- **Engagement rate**: Likes, comments, shares per post
