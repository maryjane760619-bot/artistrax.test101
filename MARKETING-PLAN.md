# artistrax Marketing Plan
## Budget: $500 | Timeline: 4 weeks

---

## Core Value Propositions

### For Artists
- **95% revenue share** (vs Spotify $0.003/stream, Beatport 70%, Bandcamp 82%)
- **Own your music AND stream it** - fans buy once, stream forever
- **30-day free trial** - then $20/mo or $96/year (save 60%)
- **Direct fan connection** - no middleman, instant payouts
- **Link-in-Bio included** - replace Linktree, centralize your presence

### For Labels
- **90% revenue share** - better than any competitor
- **Manage entire catalog** - batch upload, release management
- **Artist collaboration** - tracks can belong to both label AND artist
- **30-day free trial** - then $25/mo or $120/year (save 60%)
- **Professional tools** - analytics, downloads tracking, revenue reports

### For Fans
- **Own music forever** - not rent access like Spotify
- **Stream your purchases** - buy once, stream unlimited + download lossless
- **2% cashback rewards** - 10 points per dollar, 500 points = free track
- **Support artists directly** - 95% goes to creator
- **PWA app** - install in 10 seconds, works offline, no app store

## Marketing Budget Breakdown ($500)

### Paid Advertising ($300)
1. **Facebook/Instagram Ads** ($200)
   - Target: Electronic music producers, DJs, independent artists
   - Age: 18-45
   - Interests: Ableton, FL Studio, electronic music production, Bandcamp, Beatport
   - Campaign: "Artists keep 95%" + "Stream Your Purchases" feature
   - Goal: 500-1,000 artist signups

2. **Reddit Ads** ($50)
   - Subreddits: r/electronicmusic, r/WeAreTheMusicMakers, r/edmproduction
   - Post: "New platform pays artists 95% + lets fans stream their purchases forever"
   - Goal: Organic discussion + signups

3. **Google Ads** ($50)
   - Search: "Bandcamp alternative", "sell music online", "music distribution platform"
   - Goal: Capture artists actively looking for alternatives

### Organic & PR ($100)
1. **Press Releases** ($50)
   - Hypebot, DJ Mag, Resident Advisor, Electronic Beats
   - Angle: "Siesta Records launches fair-pay music platform"
   - Historic catalog angle (60-70 releases never digital before)

2. **Blog Outreach** ($25)
   - Electronic music blogs, DJ blogs, music tech blogs
   - Pitch: Interview with Bert about artistrax vision
   - Sample tracks from Siesta catalog

3. **Influencer Outreach** ($25)
   - EDM YouTubers, music production influencers
   - Offer: Free artist account + promo code for followers
   - Goal: Video reviews, mentions

### Launch Materials ($100)
1. **Social Media Graphics** ($40)
   - Instagram posts/stories templates
   - Artist testimonial cards
   - Feature highlight graphics (95%, Stream Your Purchases, PWA)

2. **Launch Video** ($40)
   - 60-second explainer video

3. **Email Templates** ($20)

## Risk Mitigation

### Potential Issues
1. **Low artist signups**: Increase ad spend on artist-focused campaigns
2. **Technical problems**: Have developer on standby for launch day
3. **Negative feedback**: Address publicly and transparently
4. **Press ignores launch**: Focus on organic social + paid ads

### Backup Plans
- If paid ads underperform: Shift budget to influencer partnerships
- If organic growth is strong: Reduce paid ads, invest in community
- If press coverage weak: Create own content (blog, YouTube)
