## Common Questions

### "How does the 30-day trial work?"
Sign up with your payment info. **No charge for 30 days.** Full access to everything. After 30 days, you're charged $25/mo (or $120 if you choose annual). Cancel anytime during trial with no charge.

### "Can I cancel anytime?"
**Yes.** Cancel your subscription anytime. Your catalog stays live for 30 days after cancellation, then gets archived (can reactivate by resubscribing).

### "Why $25/mo instead of free?"
artistrax is a professional platform built for labels:
- **Hosting**: Streaming + lossless downloads = bandwidth costs
- **Payment processing**: Stripe charges 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction
- **Platform development**: We build tools that help labels scale

$25/mo (or $10/mo annual) keeps the platform sustainable. Plus you keep **90%** of sales. Compare: Beatport is commission-based and takes 30% (you keep 70%). On a $100 sale, you make $90 on artistrax vs $70 on Beatport.

### "Why 90% instead of 100%?"
We take 10% to cover hosting, bandwidth, payment processing, and platform improvements. 90% is the highest revenue share for labels in the industry.

### "Do we need to be exclusive?"
**No.** List on artistrax AND Beatport AND Bandcamp. We don't lock you in.

### "How do artist splits work?"
You define the split with your artists (e.g., 50/50, 60/40, etc.). We just handle the math and payouts automatically.

### "Can we migrate our existing catalog?"
**Yes.** Use batch upload to bring over your entire catalog. We'll work with you if you have hundreds of tracks.

### "What about ISRC codes and metadata?"
Full metadata support:
- ISRC codes
- Catalog numbers
- Release dates
- Credits (producer, remixer, mastering, etc.)
- BPM, key, genre

### "Can we create sub-labels?"
Currently: Use your main label account and organize by genre/era.
Roadmap: Sub-label feature coming in Q2 2026.

### "What file formats do you accept?"
- **Upload**: WAV, FLAC, MP3 (320kbps minimum)
- **Fans download**: WAV or FLAC (lossless)
- **Fans stream**: MP3 320kbps (auto-transcoded)

### "How do payouts work?"
- Stripe Connect (same as Bandcamp)
- Label gets 90%, artist gets their % (you define split)
- Instant payouts available
- Withdraw anytime (no minimum, no holds)
