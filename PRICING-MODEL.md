# artistrax Pricing Model
## Final Pricing Structure (Confirmed)

---

## 🎨 Artists

### Pricing
- **30-day free trial** (no charge, full access)
- **Monthly:** $20/mo ($240/year)
- **Annual:** $96/year (**save 60%** = only $8/month)

### What's Included
- ✅ Upload unlimited tracks
- ✅ Keep **95%** of every sale (we take 5%)
- ✅ Advanced analytics (downloads, streams, revenue, geo data)
- ✅ Priority support
- ✅ Custom branding (avatar, bio, colors)
- ✅ Link-in-Bio feature (13 platforms)
- ✅ Instant payouts via Stripe
- ✅ Public artist page (artistrax.com/yourname)

### Trial Details
- Sign up with payment info (card required)
- **No charge for 30 days**
- Full access to everything
- After 30 days: auto-charged $20 (or $96 if annual)
- Cancel anytime during trial = no charge

### After Trial
- Stay subscribed = keep uploading and selling
- Cancel = music stays live for 30 days, then archived
- Reactivate anytime = pay to restore access

## ❓ Common Questions

### "Why charge artists/labels anything?"
**Answer:** Platform costs (hosting, streaming bandwidth, payment processing, development). $20/mo is sustainable and fair. Plus you keep 95% of sales. Compare: Bandcamp is free but takes 18% (you keep 82%). On $100 in sales, you make $95 with artistrax vs $82 with Bandcamp.

### "Can I cancel anytime?"
**Answer:** Yes. Cancel during trial = no charge. Cancel after trial = music stays live for 30 days, then archived. Reactivate anytime.

### "What happens if I don't pay?"
**Answer:** Your music stays live for 30 days after subscription ends, then gets archived (hidden from public). No data loss. Reactivate by resubscribing.

### "Can I switch from monthly to annual?"
**Answer:** Yes! Switch anytime and save 60%.

### "Do fans pay to use artistrax?"
**Answer:** No. Fans use artistrax 100% free. They only pay for the music they buy.

### "What if I sell $1000 in music?"
**Answer (Artist):** You keep 95% = $950. We take 5% = $50 to cover hosting, streaming, and payment processing.

**Answer (Label):** You keep 90% = $900. We take 10% = $100.

### "Is the 30-day trial really free?"
**Answer:** Yes! Enter your payment info at signup, but you're not charged for 30 days. Cancel before day 30 = no charge ever.

### "Can I use artistrax AND Bandcamp?"
**Answer:** Yes! Non-exclusive. List on artistrax, Bandcamp, Beatport—wherever you want.
