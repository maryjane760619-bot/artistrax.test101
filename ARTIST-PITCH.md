## Common Questions

### "How does the 30-day trial work?"
Sign up with your payment info. **No charge for 30 days.** Full access to everything. After 30 days, you're charged $20/mo (or $96 if you choose annual). Cancel anytime during trial with no charge.

### "Can I cancel anytime?"
**Yes.** Cancel your subscription anytime. Your music stays live for 30 days after cancellation, then gets archived (can reactivate by resubscribing).

### "Why $20/mo instead of free?"
artistrax is a professional platform with costs:
- **Hosting**: Audio streaming + lossless downloads = bandwidth
- **Payment processing**: Stripe charges 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction
- **Platform development**: We build features that help you succeed

$20/mo (or $8/mo annual) keeps the platform sustainable. Plus you keep **95%** of sales. Compare: Bandcamp is free but takes 18% (you keep 82%). On a $100 sale, you make $95 on artistrax vs $82 on Bandcamp.

### "Why 95% instead of 100%?"
We take 5% to cover hosting, bandwidth, payment processing, and platform improvements. 95% is the highest revenue share in the industry.

### "Do I need to be exclusive?"
**No.** List on artistrax AND Bandcamp AND Beatport. We don't lock you in.

### "What about copyrighted samples?"
You're responsible for clearing samples. Same as any platform.

### "Can I sell full albums/EPs?"
Yes! You can bundle tracks into releases with bundle pricing.

### "What file formats do you accept?"
- **Upload**: WAV, FLAC, MP3 (320kbps minimum)
- **Fans download**: WAV or FLAC (lossless)
- **Fans stream**: MP3 320kbps (auto-transcoded)

### "How do payouts work?"
- Stripe Connect (same as Bandcamp)
- Instant payouts available
- Withdraw anytime (no minimum)
- 2-3 day bank transfer

### "What if I want to remove my music later?"
You can delete tracks anytime. Your music, your control.
