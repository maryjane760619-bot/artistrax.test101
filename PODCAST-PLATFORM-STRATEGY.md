## 💰 Monetization Models (Multiple Options)

### Option 1: Pay-Per-Episode (Like Music)

**Podcaster uploads episode:**
- Sets price: $0.99, $1.99, $2.99, $3.99, or free
- Listener buys once, owns forever
- Can stream unlimited times

**Example:**
- Episode costs $1.99
- Podcaster gets 95% = $1.89
- Listener owns + streams forever

**Best for:** Evergreen content, premium interviews, courses

---

### Option 2: Podcast Subscription (Like Patreon)

**Podcaster offers monthly subscription:**
- $5/mo, $10/mo, or custom price
- Listener gets access to all episodes (new + old)
- Can download + stream unlimited

**Example:**
- Podcast costs $10/mo
- Podcaster gets 95% = $9.50/mo per subscriber
- 100 subscribers = $950/mo income

**Best for:** Ongoing shows, weekly episodes, communities

---

### Option 3: Freemium Model

**Podcaster offers:**
- Free episodes (first 10 minutes, or older episodes)
- Premium episodes ($1.99 each or $10/mo subscription)

**Example:**
- Teaser episodes free (build audience)
- Full episodes behind paywall
- Both models available

**Best for:** Building audience, testing monetization

---

### Option 4: Tips/Donations

**Listener can tip:**
- One-time: $1, $5, $10, $20, custom
- Recurring: $5/mo ongoing support (no episode access required)
- Podcaster keeps 95%

**Best for:** Free podcasts that want optional support

---

### Option 5: Bundle Pricing

**Podcaster bundles:**
- Entire season: $19.99 (10 episodes, 50% off)
- Best of collection: $9.99 (5 episodes curated)
- Back catalog: $49.99 (100+ episodes)

**Best for:** Long-running shows, archives

## 🛠️ Technical Implementation

### Infrastructure Needs

**Video storage:**
- Supabase Storage (or Backblaze B2 for cheaper)
- 2GB max per video (HD 1080p, 1-2 hours)
- Transcoding: 1080p, 720p, 480p (adaptive streaming)

**Costs:**
- Storage: $0.02/GB/month (Supabase) or $0.005/GB (Backblaze)
- Bandwidth: $0.09/GB (Supabase) or $0.01/GB (Backblaze)
- Transcoding: $0.01/minute (AWS MediaConvert or Mux)

**Example:**
- 1 hour video (1080p) = ~1GB file
- Storage: $0.02/month
- Transcoding: $0.60 one-time
- Streaming (100 views): $9 bandwidth
- **Total Year 1 cost per episode:** ~$10

**Revenue per episode:**
- 100 sales × $2.99 = $299 GMV
- Platform fee (5%): $14.95
- Subscription ($25/mo): $25

**Profit per episode:** $14.95 + $25 - $10 = **$29.95** ✅

## 🚧 Risks & Mitigation

### Risk 1: Video Storage Costs High

**Mitigation:**
- Start with audio-only (cheaper)
- Add video in Phase 2
- Use cheaper storage (Backblaze B2)
- Limit video length (2 hours max)

---

### Risk 2: Low Adoption (Podcasters Don't Switch)

**Mitigation:**
- Target music podcasters first (already on platform)
- Offer migration help (import from YouTube, Spotify)
- Case studies (show economics comparison)
- Partner with 5-10 popular podcasters as early adopters

---

### Risk 3: Technical Complexity

**Mitigation:**
- Build MVP (audio + basic video player)
- Iterate based on feedback
- Use proven tech (HLS streaming, Supabase, etc.)
