## 📰 Press Release Variants

### Variant 1: Electronic Music Focus

**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

**Siesta Records Launches artistrax: Electronic Music Platform Where Artists Keep 95%**

*New platform reimagines music ownership with "Stream Your Purchases" feature*

ENCINITAS, CA — [DATE] — Siesta Records today announced the launch of artistrax, a music distribution platform built specifically for electronic music producers. Artists keep 95% of revenue (vs Beatport's 70% and Bandcamp's 82%), and fans can own music forever while also streaming it unlimited times.

"Electronic music producers are tired of streaming pennies and unfair splits," said Bert Porcayo, CEO of Siesta Records and founder of artistrax. "We built artistrax to give artists what they deserve: 95% of every sale, instant payouts, and fans who actually own their music."

To mark the launch, Siesta Records is releasing its entire back catalog — 60-70 releases spanning 15+ years — on artistrax. This is the first time these releases have been available digitally.

For more information, visit artistrax.com or contact support@artistrax.com.

### Variant 2: Technology Focus

**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

**artistrax Launches PWA Music Platform with "Stream Your Purchases" Feature**

*No app store, 10-second install, works offline*

ENCINITAS, CA — [DATE] — artistrax today launched a Progressive Web App (PWA) music platform that installs in under 10 seconds and works offline. Unlike native apps (Spotify, Apple Music), artistrax requires no app store download and weighs less than 5MB.

The platform's unique "Stream Your Purchases" feature lets fans buy music once and stream it unlimited times, combining the ownership model of Bandcamp with the convenience of streaming.

Artists keep 95% of revenue, the highest in the industry. The platform uses Stripe Connect for instant payouts and automatic 1099-K tax reporting.

For more information, visit artistrax.com or contact support@artistrax.com.
