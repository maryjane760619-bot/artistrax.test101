## Slide 2: The Problem

### Streaming Killed Fair Pay

**Artists get pennies:**
- Spotify: $0.003 per stream
- Need 333 streams to make $1
- 50,000 streams = $150

**Download stores take too much:**
- Beatport: 30% commission (artists keep 70%)
- Bandcamp: 18% commission (artists keep 82%)

**Fans don't own anything:**
- Streaming = renting access
- Cancel subscription = lose everything
- No offline, no ownership

**The industry is broken. Artists and fans both lose.**

*Where an artist can be an artist*
