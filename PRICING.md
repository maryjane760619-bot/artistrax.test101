# Artistrax Pricing & Revenue Structure

## Revenue Splits

### Artists
- **95% to Artist** / 5% to Platform

### Labels  
- **90% to Label** / 10% to Platform

## Subscription Pricing

### Artists
- **Monthly:** $20/month
- **Annual:** $96/year (save $144/year)
- **Free Trial:** 30 days

### Labels
- **Monthly:** $25/month
- **Annual:** $120/year (save $180/year)
- **Free Trial:** 30 days

## Storage
- **Included:** 10GB
- **Unlimited:** +$5/month

## Fans
- **Free to join** — no subscription required
- **Buy tracks individually** — artist/label sets price (typically $0.99-$2.99)
- **Unlimited streaming** — of purchased tracks, forever
- **Lossless downloads** — WAV, FLAC, or MP3

## Stripe Connect
- Instant payouts to connected account
- Platform fee automatically deducted
- Remainder (90% or 95%) available for withdrawal
