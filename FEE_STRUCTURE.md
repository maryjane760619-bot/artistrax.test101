# Artistrax Updated Fee Structure

## Platform Fees Summary

| Feature | Fee | Notes |
|---------|-----|-------|
| **Track Sales (Artists)** | 5% | 95% to artist |
| **Track Sales (Labels)** | 10% | 90% to label |
| **Fan Subscriptions** | 10% | 90% to creator |
| **Live Streaming** | **10%** | Billed separately, 10% platform fee added |

---

## Live Streaming with Cloudflare Stream + 10% Fee

### Cloudflare Stream Pricing
- **Storage:** $5 per 1,000 minutes (~$0.005/minute)
- **Delivery:** $1 per 1,000 minutes (~$0.001/minute)
- **NO egress fees**

### Artist Streaming Cost Examples

| Scenario | Hours | Viewers | Minutes | Cloudflare Cost | Artistrax 10% Fee | **Total Artist Cost** |
|----------|-------|---------|---------|-----------------|-------------------|----------------------|
| Small DJ set | 2 hrs | 50 | 6,000 | $6.00 | $0.60 | **$6.60** |
| Medium show | 4 hrs | 100 | 24,000 | $24.00 | $2.40 | **$26.40** |
| Big event | 4 hrs | 500 | 120,000 | $120.00 | $12.00 | **$132.00** |

## Recommended Streaming Pricing Model

### Option B: Bundled Hours (Recommended)
- Base: $25/month includes 10 hours streaming
- Overage: $3/hour (covers Cloudflare cost + 10% fee)
- **Example - 15 hours:** $25 + (5 × $3) = **$40/month**

**Artistrax profit on 15 hour streamer: $4/month**
