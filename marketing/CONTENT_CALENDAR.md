## Content Repurposing Strategy

### Blog Post → Multiple Formats
1. **Original:** Long-form blog (2,000 words)
2. **Social:** 5-10 quote graphics
3. **Video:** 5-min explainer
4. **Email:** Digest version (500 words)
5. **Podcast:** Interview/discussion
6. **Infographic:** Key stats/visuals

### Video → Multi-Platform
1. **YouTube:** Full video (10 min)
2. **Instagram:** Clips/Reels (3x 60 sec)
3. **TikTok:** Short tips (5x 30 sec)
4. **Twitter:** Thread summary
5. **Blog:** Transcript + screenshots

## Content Production Workflow

### Weekly Sprint
**Monday:** Content planning meeting
**Tuesday-Wednesday:** Create content (write, design, film)
**Thursday:** Edit and approve
**Friday:** Schedule and publish

### Tools
- **Writing:** Notion, Grammarly
- **Design:** Canva, Figma
- **Video:** Final Cut, Descript
- **Scheduling:** Buffer, Later
- **Analytics:** Google Analytics, social native

### Approval Process
1. Create → Content Manager
2. Review → Marketing Lead  
3. Final approval → Founder (for major pieces)
4. Publish → Scheduled or immediate
