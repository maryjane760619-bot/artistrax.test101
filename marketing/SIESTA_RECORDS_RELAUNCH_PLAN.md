## Risk Mitigation

### Potential Issues & Solutions

**Low demo submissions:**
- Solution: Increase social media push, offer feedback to all submitters, extend deadline

**Back catalog underperforms:**
- Solution: Bundle deals, limited-time discounts, playlist pitching

**Technical issues with platform:**
- Solution: Have backup distribution ready, communicate transparently with fans

**Competition from bigger labels:**
- Solution: Lean into "independent" and "artist-first" messaging, community building
