# Artistrax Marketing Plan 2024

## Executive Summary

Artistrax is an electronic music download platform offering the fairest revenue splits in the industry: **95% to artists, 90% to labels**. With monthly payouts, lossless downloads, and unlimited streaming for fans, we're building the future of independent music distribution.

---

## Target Markets

### Primary: Independent Electronic Music Artists
- **Demographics:** 18-35, tech-savvy, SoundCloud/Bandcamp users
- **Pain Points:** 30-50% platform fees, delayed payouts, lack of fan data
- **Value Prop:** Keep 95%, monthly payouts, own your audience

### Secondary: Small Independent Labels  
- **Demographics:** 2-20 artists per label, genre-focused
- **Pain Points:** Complex royalty splits, manual accounting, platform lock-in
- **Value Prop:** Keep 90%, batch management, automatic payouts

### Tertiary: Electronic Music Fans
- **Demographics:** 25-40, audiophiles, vinyl collectors, club-goers
- **Pain Points:** Streaming doesn't support artists, compressed audio
- **Value Prop:** Own lossless files, 2% rewards on purchases, 90-95% goes to artist

---

## Pricing Strategy

### Artist Tier
- **Monthly:** $20/month
- **Annual:** $96/year (60% discount)
- **Split:** 95% to artist / 5% to platform
- **Trial:** 30 days free
- **Storage:** 10GB included, +$5/month unlimited

### Label Tier
- **Monthly:** $25/month  
- **Annual:** $120/year (60% discount)
- **Split:** 90% to label / 10% to platform
- **Trial:** 30 days free
- **Storage:** 10GB included, +$5/month unlimited

### Fan Purchases
- **Pricing:** Artist/Label sets price (suggested $0.99-$2.99 per track)
- **Format:** WAV, FLAC, MP3 (fan's choice)
- **Streaming:** Unlimited, forever, any device

## Competitive Positioning

### vs Bandcamp
- **Advantage:** Better split (95% vs 85%), monthly payouts vs monthly
- **Differentiator:** Built for electronic music specifically

### vs DistroKid/Amuse
- **Advantage:** Direct sales vs streaming only, keep 95% vs 100% fee
- **Differentiator:** Fan ownership, lossless downloads

### vs Beatport
- **Advantage:** Open to all artists, not curated gatekeeping
- **Differentiator:** Fair split (95% vs 50%)

### vs Self-hosted (Shopify + Stripe)
- **Advantage:** Built-in audience, streaming, no setup
- **Differentiator:** Music-specific features, discovery

## Risk Mitigation

### Risk: Low Artist Adoption
**Mitigation:**
- Aggressive free trial (30 days)
- Revenue guarantee for first 100 artists
- Heavy influencer partnerships
- Superior product experience

### Risk: Fan Acquisition
**Mitigation:**
- Lead with artist marketing (fans follow artists)
- SEO for "buy electronic music"
- Partnerships with music blogs
- Curated playlists/discovery features

### Risk: Pricing Pushback
**Mitigation:**
- Emphasize ROI (keep 95% vs 70% elsewhere)
- Annual discount (save $144-180/year)
- Success stories of profitable artists
- Grandfather early adopters

### Risk: Technical Issues
**Mitigation:**
- Beta period with forgiving users
- Fast support response (< 4 hours)
- Public status page
- Regular platform updates
