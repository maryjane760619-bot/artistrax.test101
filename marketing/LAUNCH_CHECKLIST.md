## FAQ for Support

### Pricing
**Q: Why do you charge a subscription?**
A: We don't take a percentage of your sales (just 5% or 10% platform fee). The subscription covers our costs so we can offer you the best split in the industry.

**Q: Can I downgrade/cancel?**
A: Yes, anytime. Your tracks stay live until the end of your billing period.

### Payouts
**Q: How fast do I get paid?**
A: Stripe deposits to your bank account in 2-3 business days.

**Q: Is there a minimum payout?**
A: No. Even $1 sales get deposited immediately.

### Uploads
**Q: What file formats?**
A: WAV, FLAC, MP3 (you can offer all three).

**Q: File size limit?**
A: 500MB per track. 10GB storage included, unlimited for +$5/mo.

### Fans
**Q: What do fans get?**
A: Lossless downloads (their choice of format) + unlimited streaming forever.

**Q: Can fans stream on mobile?**
A: Yes, any device with a web browser. Native apps coming soon.
