### Content Marketing
- Blog post ideas about music industry trends
- Social media post templates

- Tips for independent musicians

### SEO
- Meta descriptions for key pages
- Blog topics for traffic

- Music industry savvy
- Focus on empowerment (artists keep 95%)
- Technical enough but not jargon-heavy
