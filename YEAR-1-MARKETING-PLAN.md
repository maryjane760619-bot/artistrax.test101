# artistrax Year 1 Marketing Plan (2026)
## Detailed Month-by-Month Strategy

**Budget:** $5,000
**Goal:** 350 artists, 50 labels, 1,000 fans by end of Year 1
**Break-even:** Q4 2026

---

## Marketing Strategy Overview

### Core Pillars

1. **Content Marketing** (Organic, $0)
2. **Paid Advertising** ($2,500)
3. **PR & Influencer** ($1,500)
4. **Community Building** ($500)
5. **Partnerships** ($500)

### Target Audiences

**Primary:** Independent electronic music producers (ages 18-45)
**Secondary:** Small electronic music labels
**Tertiary:** Electronic music fans/DJs

### Channels

1. **Facebook/Instagram Ads** (paid)
2. **Reddit** (organic + paid)
3. **Twitter/X** (organic)
4. **YouTube** (organic)
5. **Email** (owned)
6. **Discord** (community)
7. **Music Blogs** (PR)
