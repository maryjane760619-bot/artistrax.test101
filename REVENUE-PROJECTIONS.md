# artistrax Revenue Projections
## 5-Year Financial Model (2026-2030)

---

## Executive Summary

**Business Model:**
- Platform fee: 5% on artist sales, 10% on label sales
- Subscription revenue: $20/mo artists, $25/mo labels
- No fan fees (100% free for buyers)

**Market Opportunity:**
- Electronic music download market: $200-300M annually
- Target: 1-5% market share by Year 5
- Focus: Independent artists + small labels

**5-Year Goals:**
- Year 1: Prove concept ($75K GMV)
- Year 3: Break even ($750K GMV, profitable)
- Year 5: Scale ($7.5M GMV, $500K+ profit)

## Key Assumptions

### User Behavior
- **Artist retention:** 70% year-over-year
- **Avg tracks/artist:** 7-10 (growing over time)
- **Upload frequency:** 2-3 new tracks per artist per year
- **Subscription conversion:** 85% continue after trial
- **Churn rate:** 15% annually

### Sales Dynamics
- **Avg downloads per track:** 15-20/year (Year 1), growing to 30-40/year (Year 5)
- **Avg price:** $1.50 (Year 1) → $1.80 (Year 5)
- **Fan purchase frequency:** 3-5 tracks/year
- **Catalog discovery:** 10% of catalog drives 80% of sales

### Revenue Mix
- **Year 1:** 88% subscription, 12% platform fees
- **Year 5:** 90% subscription, 10% platform fees
- **Subscription is the stable base**, platform fees are bonus

### Cost Structure
- **Hosting:** Scales with users (~$2/artist/year, ~$0.10/fan/year)
- **Stripe fees:** 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction
- **Marketing:** Heavy in Year 1 ($5K), then scales with growth
- **Development:** Front-loaded ($9K Year 1), then maintenance ($1-2K/mo)
- **Support:** 1 FTE per 1,000 active artists/labels

## Risks & Mitigation

### Risk 1: Low artist adoption
**Mitigation:**
- Start with Siesta Records catalog (60-70 releases = instant catalog)
- Partner with 10-20 labels in first 6 months
- Aggressive marketing in producer communities

### Risk 2: High churn
**Mitigation:**
- Focus on artist success (analytics, marketing tools)
- Build community (Discord, events, showcases)
- Offer annual plans (lock-in, reduce churn)

### Risk 3: Price competition
**Mitigation:**
- Differentiate with features (Stream Your Purchases, PWA, Link-in-Bio)
- Emphasize fair pay (95% vs 70-82%)
- Build brand loyalty with Siesta Records credibility

### Risk 4: Tech issues
**Mitigation:**
- Use proven infrastructure (Vercel, Supabase, Stripe)
- Budget for 24/7 monitoring and support
- Redundancy and backups

### Risk 5: Legal/regulatory
**Mitigation:**
- Stripe handles tax reporting (1099-Ks)
- Terms of service cover copyright responsibility
- Consult with CPA and attorney at launch
